**Summary of Essential Responsibilities and Qualifications for Support Analyst I**

**Responsibilities:**

* Provide technical support to clients remotely and in-person via phone, email, live-chat, and in-person
* Research and implement solutions for technical problems
* Diagnose and resolve technical issues encountered by clients
* Provide advice and training to clients
* Accurately log support provided and time taken in helpdesk software
* Pick up tickets from the appropriate ticket queue
* Document solutions to technical problems in the internal knowledgebase
* Work with the team to maintain client documentation
* Work on recurring support tasks
* Participate in shift rotation to provide support for all clients

**Qualifications:**

* Degree or diploma in a computer-related program or equivalent certifications
* Previous experience in customer service is an asset
* Previous work for a Managed Service Provider is an asset
* Up-to-date IT qualification (Microsoft, Cisco, CompTIA, etc.)
* Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English
* Excellent customer service and interpersonal skills
* Attention to detail

**Knowledge:**

* Software and hardware troubleshooting
* MS Office
* Basic server or networking knowledge is an asset

**Abilities:**

* Ability to learn quickly and retain new information
* Ability to follow instructions, both written and verbal
* Ability to creatively problem solve
* Ability to work under pressure in a time-sensitive environment
* Ability to work independently and seek assistance as required
