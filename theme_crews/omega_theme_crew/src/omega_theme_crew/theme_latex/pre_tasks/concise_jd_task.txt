## Job Description Summary

**Essential Responsibilities**

* Translating user requirements into technical specifications
* Developing, testing, implementing, and documenting software programs
* Performing maintenance, troubleshooting, and fault rectification of software systems
* Ensuring code/configurations adhere to security, performance, and non-functional standards
* Evaluating new technologies and their impact on systems
* Following release management processes and applying version controls
* Assisting in interpreting and documenting client requirements
* Solving problems and making decisions within given rules
* Collaborating with functional teams and managing development processes

**Essential Qualifications**

**Foundational Proficiency**

* Creative thinking
* Building and managing relationships
* Emotional agility
* Quality Assurance and Testing
* Cloud computing
* Microservices
* Adaptability
* Learning Agility

**Intermediate Proficiency**

* Programming
* Applications Integration
* Test Driven Development
* System Development Lifecycle
* Troubleshooting
* System and Technology Integration
* Verbal & written communication skills
* Collaboration & team skills
* Analytical and problem solving skills
* Data driven decision making

**Additional Requirements**

* Typically 4-6 years of relevant experience
* Post-secondary degree in related field of study (or equivalent combination of education and experience)
